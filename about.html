About SAT

"The night is long, but it ends."

SAT (Sleep Anxiety Tracker) was built for one specific moment: Panic at 2 AM.

If you are here, you know the feeling. The world is asleep, but your mind is racing. You are counting the hours until your alarm goes off, calculating how tired you will be tomorrow. This is the "anxiety loop," and it is the primary enemy of sleep.

Our Philosophy

SAT is not a sleep tracker. We believe that tracking your sleep‚Äîmeasuring hours, REM cycles, and efficiency‚Äîoften creates more anxiety (a phenomenon known as orthosomnia).

Instead, SAT is a Panic Interrupter. It is a digital first-aid kit designed to serve a single purpose: to gently steer your brain from "fight or flight" back to a state of safety.

How It Works

The app relies on three core pillars of cognitive regulation:

  1. Physiological Regulation: Using paced breathing (like Box Breathing) to manually slow the heart rate and signal safety to the vagus nerve.
  2. Cognitive Reframing: Using simple, non-judgmental scripts to break the loop of catastrophic thinking.
  3. Cognitive Shuffling: Using random imagery to disrupt logical worry chains and mimic the brain's natural sleep onset process.

Privacy First

In an era of data mining, your sleep struggles are personal. We believe they should stay that way.

SAT is designed with Digital Minimalism and Privacy at its core:

  • No Accounts: You never need to sign up or log in.
  • Local Storage: Any journaling or settings are stored 100% locally on your device. We cannot see your data.
  • Offline Ready: The app works perfectly without an internet connection (once loaded).
